Schedule

Hi there! We hope you are enjoying our website as we do. This page is going to help you choose the best tour option for you. This is how it works: we give you some options with the main attractions and their details like eating schedule, time of departure and tour duration. The only thing you have to do is select wisely the one that you like the most.

-Option 1: If you don’t mind getting up early, this would be a great option.

At 7:00 am is your appointment for a nutritious breakfast at a famous restaurant in the center of Puntarenas. Then, at 8:00 am our bus is going to take you to Jaco Beach. There, you are not going to lay on the sand only, but also experience different aquatic activities. You are going to have lunch at 2:00 pm, but don’t worry we are going to give you snacks in the meantime. At 3:00 pm our bus is going to take you to Montezuma’s Waterfalls. You will be back around 7:00 pm.

-Option 2: Standrad

At 8:00 am you are going to have breakfast in a restaurant with a beautiful view to the Pacific Ocean. At 9:00 am our bus is going to take to Manuel Antonio National Park. There, an experienced guide will explain everything to you. You are going to eat in the park at 2:30 pm and leave at 6:00 pm in our bus. *In our last tour we were going to visit Jaco Beach too, but we experienced some delays because the National Park have many activities included, that’s why the option 2 looks shorter.

-Option 3: For the adventurous ones.

At 8:00 am our bus is going to take you closer to the port, there you are going to have your breakfast. At 9:00 am you have to take the ship to Coco Island. It will be a calm trip. When you arrive, look for a guide with our logo, he will gladly help you. You are going to visit famous spots in the island and have lots of fun. You will return at 6:00 pm.
