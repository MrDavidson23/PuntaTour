Jacó Beach

Jacó beach is one of the most famous place in Costa Rica for tourism

What can we do?

It doesn't matter if you're traveling alone, with kids, or with a large group of adrenaline-seeking adventurers. Jaco truly has something for everyone and becomes a much loved travel destination for all who visit. There is a wide variety of exciting things to do in this pleasant city, which certainly includes surfing, horseback riding, deep sea fishing, ATV tours, kayak tours, boating, rafting and much more. Jacó is such a perfect and beautiful place to relax or search for adventure. Jacó is small but their people have a big heart.
