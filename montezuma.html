Waterfalls of Montezuma

Located on the Nicoya Peninsula, Montezuma is a small beach town known for its bohemian attitude and artistic residents.

What is Montezuma?

The Waterfalls of Montezuma are one of the most emblematic and famous waterfallas of Costa Rica. They are composed of three separate waterfalls, not far from the small town of Montezuma. In the falls, you will find magic rainforests, ideal cliffs ideal for diving and a rope swing suspended in the air. But, you must consider that the weather is too hot, in the hottest day of the year temperature can exceed 38 celsius degrees. Whether with appetite for adventure or looking for serenity, you can find it in this first freshwater place. Get ready to enjoying the perfect landscape and the silent nature.
