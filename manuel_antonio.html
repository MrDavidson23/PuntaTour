Manuel Antonio

Manuel Antonio National Park is located in Quepos canton, Puntarenas province

What is Manuel Antonio?

Manuel Antonio is one of the most recognized parks in Costa Rica, many tourists around the world visit this National Park every year. It is famous because of its beautiful landscapes and diversity of living beings. It’s common to see slots, birds and white face monkeys. At the same time, it’s a perfect place for walking and exercising. Manuel Antonio has a magical weather. It mixes the hot weather of a beach and the freshness of the forest. The park offers many extra activities to the visitors, but the main attraction is to walk through the calm forest and to connect with the nature around.
