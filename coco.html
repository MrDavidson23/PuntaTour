Coco Island

The entirety of Cocos Island has been designated a Costa Rican National Park since 1978, and has no permanent inhabitants other than Costa Rican park rangers.

What is Coco Island?

Coco Island, the most important island in Costa Rica belonging to Puntarenas. This place, located in the Pacific Ocean, is a historic site that has been declared a world patrimony. It is an island where its biodiversity is completely natural because it acts as a natural reserve, it is perfect for scientific research. A beautiful place where humanity does not interrupt. Coco Island is also known for its famous maritime life, so activities such as snorkeling or diving are a magical experience.
